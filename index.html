Publications

Measuring the quality of a match

Labour Economics, Volume 215, 104753

May 2024

(with Michèle Belot and Xiaoying Liu)

In this paper, we review the advantages and drawbacks of various measures of match quality. We find that wages, performance evaluations, and measures of skill fit correlate well with each other, but not with tenure length and job satisfaction. We raise a word of caution in using a subset of measures to assess the quality of a match.

Working papers

The non-green effects of going green: Local environmental and economic consequences of lithium extraction in Chile

(with Leonardo Peñaloza-Pacheco and Gonzalo Martínez)

In this paper, we analyze the local environmental and economic impacts of lithium extraction in the Atacama Salt Flat (ASF) in Chile. We use groundwater well levels to estimate the impact of the exposure to lithium extraction on water availability. Consequently, we use satellite data to estimate the effects of this exposure on vegetation, nighttime light radiance, and local human settlements near the ASF.
